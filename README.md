# 🚀 ReachFlow - AI-Powered LinkedIn Content Platform

The easiest way to create, plan, and manage high-performing LinkedIn content!

---

## 💡 How ReachFlow Works (Step-by-Step)

### 1️⃣ **Generate a LinkedIn Post** (Post Creation Wizard)
1. **Enter your topic**: What do you want to write about? (e.g., "Building software", "Sales tips", "AI trends")
2. **Add key points**: List 2-3 key messages you want to convey
3. **Choose your audience**: Who are you targeting? (e.g., "Founders", "Sales teams", "Developers")
4. **Pick a style**: Browse 20+ reference posts in our library and select your favorite writing style
5. **Choose a hook**: Select from 10 attention-grabbing opening lines
6. **Generate!**: Click and get a full LinkedIn post tailored to your voice!

### 2️⃣ **Edit & Refine Your Post**
- **5 edit modes**: Rewrite, Shorten, Expand, Make Professional, Optimize for Viral
- **3 variations**: Story Version, Professional Version, Engaging Version
- **Copy to clipboard**: Instantly copy your final post for LinkedIn!

### 3️⃣ **Save & Track Your Posts (History)**
- All generated posts are automatically saved
- **Favorite posts**: Star your best-performing content
- **See stats**: Track how many times you've reused each post
- **Search & filter**: Find old posts quickly
- **Reuse & edit**: Load any previous post back into the editor to make changes

### 4️⃣ **Plan Your Content (Calendar)**
- **Visual calendar**: See your planned content at a glance
- **Add events**: Schedule posts, add ideas, or set tasks
- **Choose colors & categories**: Organize your content
- **Delete & edit**: Modify your calendar anytime
- **Perfect for consistency**: Plan weeks ahead and stay consistent

### 5️⃣ **Train Your Voice (Voice Calibration)**
- **Add samples**: Paste your best previous LinkedIn posts
- **Save your voice**: The AI learns your unique writing style
- **Personalized content**: All new posts will sound like *you*

### 6️⃣ **Reference Library**
- **20+ pre-built templates**: Browse high-performing LinkedIn posts
- **Organized by category**: Founder, AI, Sales, Storytelling, Marketing, Career Growth
- **Add your own**: Save your favorite posts to your personal library
- **Search & filter**: Find the perfect style quickly

### 7️⃣ **AI Comment Generator**
- Paste any LinkedIn post
- Choose your goal: Networking, Engaging, or Insightful
- Get 3 unique, high-quality comments to post!

## 🔧 Getting Started

First, set up your environment variables in `.env.local`:
```env
OPENROUTER_API_KEY=sk-or-v1-... (get your key from openrouter.ai)
NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL=...
NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=...
```

Then run the development server:

```bash
npm run dev
# or
yarn dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) and start creating!
